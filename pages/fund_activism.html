Barking at the right tree

Evidence from Activist invetors catering
Download PDF

This paper documents activists catering to mutual fund families’ preferences during proxy fights. Using funds historical shareholder proposal votes, I construct a machine learning model to measure fund’s preference in terms of text features, and subsequently predict fund voting.

What do boards say about executive compensation?

with Xiaoxiao Tang

Mutual fund families remain the largest investors in US domestic financial market for the past two decades. Through 2015 to 2018, mutual funds hold around a quarter of the shares of US-issued equities outstanding. Moreover, index mutual funds and index ETF’s continue to grow both in terms of net asset as well as fraction of mutual fund holdings.
